The Craft

01

Design Consultation

Every guitar begins with your vision. We discuss tone preferences, playing style, aesthetic desires, and technical specifications to create a blueprint for your dream instrument.

02

Wood Selection

Premium tonewoods are hand-selected for their acoustic properties and visual character. From resonant mahogany to figured maple, each piece is chosen to complement your design.

03

Body Construction

Precision cutting, shaping, and routing bring the body to life. Every contour is refined for comfort and balance, while maintaining optimal resonance and sustain.

04

Neck & Fretwork

The neck is carved to your exact specifications, with frets meticulously leveled, crowned, and polished. This ensures perfect intonation and effortless playability across every position.

05

Finishing

Multiple layers of hand-applied finish protect the wood while enhancing its natural beauty. Each coat is cured and buffed to a flawless, professional-grade sheen.

06

Electronics & Setup

Premium pickups, hardware, and electronics are installed with precision. The final setup includes action adjustment, intonation, and tone shaping to deliver exceptional performance.
